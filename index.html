About Us

Empowering Businesses with Trusted Financial Expertise

Shekar & Co. is a team of passionate Chartered Accountants and finance professionals dedicated to delivering excellence in Auditing, Taxation, Accounting, Business Advisory and CFO services, Due Diligence and Valuation Services.
Driven by a mission to simplify compliance and support business growth, we combine industry expertise, technology, and personalized strategies to deliver ethical, proactive, and growth-oriented solutions. Our focus is on building long-term client relationships and helping you grow beyond numbers
