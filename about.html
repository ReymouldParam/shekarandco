About The Founder

CA Shekar Sanda, FCA, DISA

A Fellow Chartered Accountant with more than 12 years of professional experience in Auditing, Finance and Taxation and a Diploma holder in International Taxation and Information System Audit. His academic record right from the start was excellent, which brought him an all India 27th Rank in CA Exams.
